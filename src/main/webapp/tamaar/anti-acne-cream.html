Anti Acne Cream

£79.00

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • How to use

This luxurious treatment, rich in GLA, omega-3 and antioxidants, visibly plumps and smooths the skin to help hands look and feel rejuvenated. Non-greasy and non-sticky, it glides easily onto the skin and helps to improve the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Hands are left looking firmer and feeling exceptionally soft, smooth and delicately fragranced with the divine scent of neroli, chamomile and lavender. Powerful naturally active ingredients include kigelia extract, organic rosehip oil, cranberry seed oil, borage oil, pomegranate extract and natural source vitamin E. Ideal for more mature or very dry skin.

Certified Organic by the Soil Association. Suitable for Vegans. Not tested on animals. Contains nut oil.

Eyes
Pump one dose of cleansing cream on to a cotton wool pad and gently pull the pad across the eye lid to gently lift away make-up.

Face
Gently massage 2-3 pumps of cleansing cream onto dry skin. Remove with a warm damp muslin cloth using a circular motion.

Rinse the cloth and repeat the exercise until all the cream has been removed. Finish with a splash of cold water to the face.

Storage
Store in a cool dry place. Use within 6 months of opening.
