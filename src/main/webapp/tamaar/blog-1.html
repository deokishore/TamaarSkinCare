True Beauty April 15 2015

Beauty is a state of being and it can start with a prayer of gratitude.
Being grateful activates your inner light, your sense of oneness with creation. It helps you focus on all that is good in your life therefor attracting more of that good. Gratitude spurs abundance and happiness , for in taking a little time to feel into it , the feeling starts to fill you.
I like to start the day with prayers of gratitude and love. Making a choice to focus my mind, thoughts and heart on giving thanks is a simple practise on the beauty path. When I’m feeling down focusing on the little beauties lifts me and is much nicer than dwelling on my worries. Not that I’m encouraging denying the hard stuff, sometimes we have to feel pain to grow and move through difficult feelings,  but amongst it is always beauty, sometimes the most exquisite , as the contrast is so vast.
It helps to breathe deeply, l like to begin on waking , calling upon Angels to be with me while l count my blessings. Often l like to walk , opening my breath even more and seeing the beauty of nature all around. I often notice after about half an hour of walking I feel clearer and more able to focus on the beauty , letting go the other mind chatter that arises, getting clear , walking the beauty path. 
Perhaps you’d like to try it , in whatever way works for you ?
Even just for a week , it really takes no extra time as it can be done through many daily tasks. It is just a choice of mind , breathe , heart. You can start by thinking of ten things you are grateful for when you wake up in the morning , it could be a simple appreciation of the tea you drink , your loved ones , aspects of nature you appreciate , once you start the realisation of so many simple blessings often come streaming through. It is also a helpful little mind trick when your feeling flat as our thoughts effect our experience.
All love , beauty and abundant blessings to you ,
Love Richard.
